[2;36m                    [0m         [39m### [0m[1;36m5[0m[39m. Side Effects and Mitigations[0m                                                 [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m**Potential side effects:**[0m                                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. **Normalized paths may look different in logs**: Paths will no longer contain [0m   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`..[0m[35m/[0m[39m` sequences in error messages[0m                                                   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - **Mitigation**: This is actually beneficial as it shows the actual target path[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   [0m                                                                                 [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m2[0m[39m. **Symbolic links**: The fix uses `[0m[1;35mnormalize[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` which is lexical, not [0m            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mfilesystem-aware[0m                                                                    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - **Mitigation**: This is intentional - we don't want to follow symlinks during [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mpath validation as that could introduce TOCTOU [0m[1;39m([0m[39mTime Of Check Time Of Use[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m [0m         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mvulnerabilities[0m                                                                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - **Rationale**: Using `[0m[1;35mtoRealPath[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` instead would require the parent directory[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mto exist and could resolve symlinks, which might bypass security checks[0m             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m3[0m[39m. **Performance**: Two additional `[0m[1;35mnormalize[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` calls per entry[0m                    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - **Mitigation**: Negligible impact as `[0m[1;35mnormalize[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` is a pure string operation [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mwithout I/O[0m                                                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m### [0m[1;36m6[0m[39m. Reference to Similar Solutions[0m                                               [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mThe fix follows the industry-standard approach for path traversal prevention:[0m       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m- OWASP recommends normalizing paths before validation[0m                              [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m- Java's `[0m[1;35mPath.normalize[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` specifically resolves `.` and `..` components[0m           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m- The pattern of [0m[32m"normalize then validate"[0m[39m is used throughout secure file handling [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mcode[0m                                                                                [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m### [0m[1;36m7[0m[39m. Why Other Approaches Were Not Chosen[0m                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m**Alternative: Using `[0m[1;35mtoRealPath[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m`**[0m                                               [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m**Alternative: String-based validation [0m[1;39m([0m[39mchecking for `..` in the name[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m**[0m            [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m- Path normalization is the correct abstraction for this problem[0m                    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m**Alternative: Modifying [0m[1;35mExpander.expand[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m**[0m                                        [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m- The security check belongs in the `[0m[1;35mresolveIn[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` method as it's the central [0m       [2m                [0m
