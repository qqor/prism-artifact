[2;36m                    [0m         [39m## Fix Strategy[0m                                                                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m<fix_strategy>[0m                                                                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. Identify root cause:[0m                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - In exif_data_load_data [0m[1;39m([0m[39mlibexif/exif-data.c[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m, the code reads the EXIF APP1 [0m    [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m923[0m[39m–[0m[1;36m929[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m, then later clamps only to [0m[1;36m0xfffe[0m[39m [0m[1;39m([0m[39mlines [0m[1;36m952[0m[39m–[0m[1;36m958[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m. It never verifies that [0m [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m2[0m[39m. Preserve existing behavior:[0m                                                      [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - We must keep the same `CHECKOVERFLOW`-based guards and the IFD‐entry parsing [0m  [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - We must not touch harness or fuzzing code.[0m                                     [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Tests or valid images that declare a correct header length remain unaffected.[0m  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
