[2;36m                    [0m         [39m## Fix Strategy[0m                                                                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m<fix_strategy>[0m                                                                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. Overview of the problem  [0m                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   The fuzzer discovered that when Tika’s R unpacker emits embedded files, a [0m       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mmaliciously crafted entry name containing “..” segments [0m[1;39m([0m[39me.g. [0m                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m“..[0m[35m/../../[0m[95mjazzer-traversal[0m[39m”[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m flows unchecked into `FileSystemEmitter.emit`.  [0m       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mBecause `[0m[1;35mbasePath.resolve[0m[1;39m([0m[39mpath[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` is called without normalization or validation, the[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39memitter will happily write outside of its intended directory.  Jazzer’s [0m            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`FilePathTraversal` sanitizer catches this and crashes the process.[0m                 [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m2[0m[39m. Core insight and root cause  [0m                                                    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   – In `FileSystemEmitter.emit`, the string `path` [0m[1;39m([0m[39mderived from embedded-document[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mmetadata[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m is handed straight to `[0m[1;35mbasePath.resolve[0m[1;39m([0m[39mpath[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m`.  [0m                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   – No normalization or containment check is performed, so “..” segments may [0m      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mescape `basePath`.  [0m                                                                [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   – Although `AbstractEmbeddedDocumentBytesHandler.addSubDirs` does a weak [0m        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mregex-based cleanup, it does not cover all path‐traversal variants [0m[1;39m([0m[39mWindows [0m        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mbackslashes, leading “..”, mixed separators, etc.[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m.  [0m                               [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m3[0m[39m. Fix location  [0m                                                                   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   Patch `[0m[1;35morg.apache.tika.pipes.emitter.fs.FileSystemEmitter.emit[0m[1;39m([0m[33m...[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m`, the single[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mcentralized sink that writes to disk.  By normalizing and validating the [0m           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39membedded‐path string *before* any filesystem operations, we can guarantee no write [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mwill ever escape `basePath`.[0m                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m4[0m[39m. Detailed patch steps  [0m                                                           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   In `FileSystemEmitter.java`, modify the first few lines of the `emit` method as [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mfollows:[0m                                                                            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   a. Parse and normalize the incoming `path` into a `java.nio.file.Path`.  [0m        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   b. Reject or skip any normalized path that is either absolute or begins with a [0m  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mparent‐directory reference [0m[1;39m([0m[39m`..`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m.  [0m                                                [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   c. Only after it passes validation, resolve it against `basePath` and proceed as[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mnormal.[0m                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   Pseudo‐code change:[0m                                                              [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   ```[0m                                                                              [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m5[0m[39m. Why this approach  [0m                                                              [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   – **Security**: By canonicalizing and validating *before* every write, we close [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mthe path‐traversal hole once and for all.  [0m                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   – **Simplicity**: Centralized in the emitter; no need to patch multiple handlers[0m [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39munaffected because, after `[0m[1;35m.normalize[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m`, they neither start with “..” nor are [0m     [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39mPaths that would have written outside `basePath` are simply not emitted, preventing[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mJazzer from tripping on file‐system writes outside the sandbox.[0m                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m6[0m[39m. Side effects & mitigation  [0m                                                      [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39mthe logs.  [0m                                                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   – **Behavioral consistency**: This only changes handling of *unsafe* paths; [0m     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mnormal extraction behavior is preserved.  [0m                                          [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m7[0m[39m. [0m[1;39m([0m[39mOptional[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m Upstream hygiene  [0m                                                    [2m                [0m
