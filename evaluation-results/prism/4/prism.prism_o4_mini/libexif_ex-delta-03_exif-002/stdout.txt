[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. Overview of the problem  [0m                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   The heap‐buffer‐overflow in exif_data_load_data_entry stems from an [0m             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mout‐of‐bounds “size” parameter being handed down from exif_data_load_data.  In [0m     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mAPP1‐segment parsing, the code unconditionally overwrites its local `ds` [0m[1;39m([0m[39mthe [0m      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m“remaining bytes” count[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m with the value `len - [0m[1;36m2[0m[39m` from the JPEG/EXIF length field [0m  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mand only caps it at [0m[1;36m0xfffe[0m[39m.  It never ensures that `len - [0m[1;36m2[0m[39m` is ≤ the actual [0m       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mbuffer‐length passed in.  Consequently, downstream functions [0m[1;39m([0m[39mincluding [0m            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mexif_data_load_data_entry[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m believe there is more data available than exists in [0m     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mmemory, so their memcpy bounds checks [0m[1;39m([0m[39m`doff < size`, `s ≤ size - doff`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m succeed [0m   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39meven though the real buffer is shorter—leading to an ASan‐reported overflow on [0m     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mreads from the fuzzer’s input buffer.[0m                                               [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m2[0m[39m. Patch location  [0m                                                                 [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   In libexif/exif-data.c, inside the function `exif_data_load_data` where APP1 [0m    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39msegments are handled [0m[1;39m([0m[39maround lines [0m[1;36m921[0m[39m–[0m[1;36m928[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m.[0m                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m3[0m[39m. Pseudocode of the patch  [0m                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   a. At the very top of the APP1‐handling block, save the incoming `ds` as [0m        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`orig_ds`.  [0m                                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   b. Before you set `ds = len - [0m[1;36m2[0m[39m`, do minimal sanity checks: ensure `orig_ds` is [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mlarge enough to consume the marker plus length‐field, and that `len >= [0m[1;36m2[0m[39m`.  If not,[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mquietly bail out of parsing the segment.  [0m                                          [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   c. After `ds = len - [0m[1;36m2[0m[39m`, clamp `ds` so that it never exceeds the real available [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mbytes [0m[1;39m([0m[39m`orig_ds − bytes_consumed_to_read_len`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m.  [0m                                   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   d. Then apply the existing `[0m[1;36m0xfffe[0m[39m` cap.  [0m                                       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m     ```[0m                                                                            [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m     if [0m[1;39m([0m[39morig_ds < [0m[1;36m3[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m [0m[1;39m{[0m                                                             [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   e. Leave the rest of the function [0m[1;39m([0m[39mincluding the call to [0m                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`[0m[1;35mexif_data_load_data_content[0m[1;39m([0m[39md+[0m[1;36m6[0m[39m, ds-[0m[1;36m6[0m[39m, …[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m unchanged.  [0m                           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m4[0m[39m. Why this fixes the overflow  [0m                                                    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - By recording the original `ds` [0m[1;39m([0m[39mactual buffer length[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m and ensuring `len-[0m[1;36m2[0m[39m` [0m    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mnever exceeds `orig_ds - [0m[1;36m3[0m[39m`, we guarantee that any downstream “size” parameters [0m    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;39m([0m[39m`ds-[0m[1;36m6[0m[39m`, then passed into load_data_entry[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m can never lie beyond the real buffer.  [0m  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - The existing checks in exif_data_load_data_entry [0m[1;39m([0m[39m`doff < size; s <= size - [0m   [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - We still cap at [0m[1;36m0xfffe[0m[39m to defend against ridiculously large length fields.[0m     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m5[0m[39m. Preserving existing functionality  [0m                                              [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Valid EXIF APP1 segments [0m[1;39m([0m[39mwhere the length field matches the actual data [0m      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mlength[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m are parsed exactly as before.  [0m                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Malformed or truncated segments simply abort parsing that segment, leaving the[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mlibrary in a clean state [0m[1;39m([0m[39mno buffer overflow[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m.  The rest of the JPEG parsing [0m[1;39m([0m[39mother[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39msegments, error cleanup[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m is unchanged.  [0m                                            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - No changes are made to fuzz harnesses, tests, or external libraries.  The [0m     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mfuzzer will simply no longer crash on this particular out‐of‐bounds path.[0m           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m6[0m[39m. Potential side effects and mitigation  [0m                                          [2m                [0m
