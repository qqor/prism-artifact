[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. Root Cause Analysis  [0m                                                            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - The `Expander` class‚Äôs ZIP-specific expand method [0m[1;39m([0m[39mlines [0m[1;36m470[0m[39m‚Äì[0m[1;36m497[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m delegates [0m   [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39mtree[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m but blocks any traversal via symlink jumps.[0m                                   [2m                [0m
