[2;36m                    [0m         [39mseconds. The global `Curl_seconds` array only has [0m[1;36m60[0m[39m entries [0m[1;39m([0m[39mindices [0m[1;36m0[0m[39m–[0m[1;36m59[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m. Thus, [0m [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m2[0m[39m. Preservation of Existing Functionality[0m                                           [2m                [0m
