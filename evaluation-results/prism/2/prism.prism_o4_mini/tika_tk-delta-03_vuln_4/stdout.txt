[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. Root cause identification  [0m                                                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   The SSRF issue is triggered because [0m[1;35mThreeDXMLParser.parseRoot[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m creates a raw [0m   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mSAXParser via `[0m[1;35mSAXParserFactory.newInstance[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` without disabling external‐entity or[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mDTD resolution. As a result, any DOCTYPE declarations or external entity references[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39min the XML can cause the parser to open [0m[1;35mHTTP[0m[1;39m([0m[39mS[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m connections [0m[1;39m([0m[39me.g., to [0m              [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m“jazzer.com:[0m[1;36m443[0m[39m”[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m, leading to SSRF findings under the Jazzer sanitizer.[0m             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m2[0m[39m. Leverage existing secure parsing utilities  [0m                                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   Apache Tika’s XMLReaderUtils already provides hardened parsing routines that [0m    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mdisable external DTDs/entities and enforce secure‐processing. ThreeDXMLParser [0m      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mitself uses `[0m[1;35mXMLReaderUtils.parseSAX[0m[1;39m([0m[33m...[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` for parsing the Manifest.xml; the same [0m  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mutility should be reused for parsing the “root” XML.[0m                                [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m3[0m[39m. Detailed patch steps  [0m                                                           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   a. In ThreeDXMLParser.parseRoot [0m[1;39m([0m[39mlines [0m[1;36m123[0m[39m–[0m[1;36m143[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m, remove the block that manually [0m [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   b. Replace it with a call to `[0m[1;35mXMLReaderUtils.parseSAX[0m[1;39m([0m[39mInputStream, [0m              [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`context`.  [0m                                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   c. Import `org.apache.tika.utils.XMLReaderUtils` and remove any now‐unused [0m      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mimports [0m[1;39m([0m[39m`SAXParserFactory`, `SAXParser`, `ParserConfigurationException`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m.  [0m        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   d. Ensure the method signature [0m[1;39m([0m[39m`throws SAXException, IOException, [0m              [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mTikaException`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m remains unchanged, since `parseSAX` throws exactly those exception [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mtypes.[0m                                                                              [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m4[0m[39m. Rationale and safety  [0m                                                           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   – By reusing `XMLReaderUtils.parseSAX`, the parser factory is configured with [0m   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39msecure features:  [0m                                                                  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m     • External-general-entities disabled  [0m                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m     • External-parameter-entities disabled  [0m                                       [2m                [0m
