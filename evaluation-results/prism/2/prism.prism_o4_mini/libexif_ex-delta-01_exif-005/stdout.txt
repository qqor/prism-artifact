[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Reuse the existing `[0m[1;35mCHECKOVERFLOW[0m[1;39m([0m[39moff, buf_len, size[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` macro if available; [0m    [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m5[0m[39m. Justification  [0m                                                                  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - This approach confines changes strictly to the Apple maker-note loader and [0m    [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - It preserves the existing parsing semantics, merely inserting safety checks [0m   [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - It directly addresses the sanitizer’s complaint by ensuring no read occurs [0m    [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - It follows the established “Fuji” pattern, unifying the library’s handling of [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mall maker-note formats.  [0m                                                           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m6[0m[39m. Side effects & mitigation  [0m                                                      [2m                [0m
