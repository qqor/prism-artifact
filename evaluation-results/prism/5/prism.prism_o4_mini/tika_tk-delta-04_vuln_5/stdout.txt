[2;36m                    [0m         [39m## Fix Strategy[0m                                                                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m<fix_strategy>[0m                                                                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. Overview of the root cause  [0m                                                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - The emitter’s `[0m[1;35memit[0m[1;39m([0m[39mString path, …[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` method in FileSystemEmitter simply does  [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m        `Path target = [0m[1;35mbasePath.resolve[0m[1;39m([0m[39mpath[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m;`  [0m                                   [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m“..[0m[35m/../../[0m[95mfoo[0m[39m” thus escapes `basePath`.  [0m                                           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Higher-level sanitization in `AbstractEmbeddedDocumentBytesHandler` [0m           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;39m([0m[39m`addSubDirs`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m uses brittle regex replacements and fails to catch many “..” [0m        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mvariants, so invalid paths reach the emitter.[0m                                       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39mconfigured `basePath`.  [0m                                                            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   B. Simplify and strengthen sub-directory path construction so that upstream [0m     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39msanitization does not introduce traversal payloads.  [0m                               [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   C. Retain existing `ON_EXISTS` semantics and directory-creation logic; add no-op[0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mbehavior when paths are valid, but fail fast on traversal attempts.  [0m               [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m3[0m[39m. Detailed steps  [0m                                                                 [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   Step [0m[1;36m1[0m[39m: In [0m[1;35mFileSystemEmitter.emit[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m  [0m                                            [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   b. Check containment:  [0m                                                          [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m        if [0m[1;39m([0m[39m![0m[1;35mnormalizedTarget.startsWith[0m[1;39m([0m[39mnormalizedBase[0m[1;39m)[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m [0m[1;39m{[0m[39m  [0m                       [2m                [0m
