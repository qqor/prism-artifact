[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. Overview & Root Causes  [0m                                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - The OOME originates in StringUtil.getFromUnicodeLE, which imposes no [0m          [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39msub‚Äêattributes, each allocating data and possibly strings.  [0m                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m2[0m[39m. Patching StringUtil.getFromUnicodeLE  [0m                                           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   a. At the top of [0m[1;35mgetFromUnicodeLE[0m[1;39m([0m[39mbyte[0m[1;39m[[0m[1;39m][0m[39m string, int offset, int len[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m, insert a [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39msanity check against StringUtil.MAX_RECORD_LENGTH.  [0m                                [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m      - e.g.  [0m                                                                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m        ```java[0m                                                                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m        if [0m[1;39m([0m[39mlen > MAX_RECORD_LENGTH[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m [0m[1;39m{[0m                                              [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m3[0m[39m. Limiting MAPI attribute counts in MAPIAttribute.create  [0m                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   a. After reading `int count = [0m[1;35mLittleEndian.readInt[0m[1;39m([0m[39minp[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m;`, validate:  [0m           [2m                [0m
