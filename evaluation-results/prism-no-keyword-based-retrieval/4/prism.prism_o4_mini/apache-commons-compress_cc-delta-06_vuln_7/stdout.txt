[2;36m                    [0m         [39m## Fix Strategy[0m                                                                     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m<fix_strategy>[0m                                                                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. Overview[0m                                                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - The root cause is that `Expander` blindly resolves archive entry names under [0m  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mthe target directory without verifying that the resulting `Path` remains within [0m    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mthat directory. A malicious entry name containing `..[0m[35m/[0m[39m` can escape the intended [0m    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mextraction root, triggering Jazzer‚Äôs `FilePathTraversal` sanitizer.[0m                 [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - We will harden the core expansion logic in the private [0m                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`[0m[1;35mexpand[0m[1;39m([0m[39mArchiveEntrySupplier<T>, ArchiveEntryBiConsumer<T>, Path[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` method by [0m       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mnormalizing each candidate output path and rejecting any that fall outside the [0m     [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mnormalized target directory.[0m                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m2[0m[39m. Location of the patch[0m                                                            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - File: [0m                                                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`src/main/java/org/apache/commons/compress/archivers/examples/Expander.java`[0m        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Method: private `<T extends ArchiveEntry> void [0m[1;35mexpand[0m[1;39m([0m[39mArchiveEntrySupplier<T> [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39msupplier, ArchiveEntryBiConsumer<T> writer, Path targetDirectory[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m` [0m[1;39m([0m[39mlines [0m[1;36m67[0m[39m‚Äì[0m[1;36m93[0m[39m in [0m [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mversion [0m[1;36m1.22[0m[39m+[0m[1;39m)[0m                                                                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m3[0m[39m. Patch steps[0m                                                                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   a. Compute and normalize each entry‚Äôs intended output path:[0m                      [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m      - Change the existing  [0m                                                       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m        `final Path targetPath = nullTarget ? null : [0m                               [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;35mnextEntry.resolveIn[0m[1;39m([0m[39mtargetDirPath[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m;`  [0m                                              [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m        to  [0m                                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m        `final Path targetPath = nullTarget ? null : [0m                               [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;35mnextEntry.resolveIn[0m[1;39m([0m[39mtargetDirPath[0m[1;39m)[0m[1;35m.normalize[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m[39m;`[0m                                    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   b. Immediately follow with a containment check:[0m                                  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m      ```[0m                                                                           [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m      if [0m[1;39m([0m[39m!nullTarget && ![0m[1;35mtargetPath.startsWith[0m[1;39m([0m[39mtargetDirPath[0m[1;39m)[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m [0m[1;39m{[0m                   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m          throw new [0m[1;35mIOException[0m[1;39m([0m[32m"Entry '"[0m[39m + [0m[1;35mnextEntry.getName[0m[1;39m([0m[1;39m)[0m                     [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`NullOutputStream` handling[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m unchanged, but now safely acting on the normalized, [0m   [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m4[0m[39m. Rationale[0m                                                                        [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Placing a single guard in the core loop protects all overloads of [0m             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m`[0m[1;35mexpand[0m[1;39m([0m[33m...[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m`, since they all eventually delegate here.[0m                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Normalizing both the target directory [0m[1;39m([0m[39m`targetDirPath`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m and the entry path [0m    [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Legitimate archives [0m[1;39m([0m[39mthose without traversal entries[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m behave exactly as [0m       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mbefore; no other functionality is altered.[0m                                          [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m5[0m[39m. Preservation of existing behavior[0m                                                [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - We only add validation and normalization; no existing branches or flows are [0m   [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39mremoved.[0m                                                                            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Null-target mode [0m[1;39m([0m[39mwriting to `[0m[35m/dev/[0m[95mnull[0m[39m` or `NUL`[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m is still bypassed entirely [0m [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Integration tests or clients that rely on `Expander` to throw on malformed [0m    [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m6[0m[39m. Potential side effects and mitigation[0m                                            [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - Archives containing entries with absolute paths or `..` segments will now fail[0m [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39mlogic in `Expander` is modified.[0m                                                    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m7[0m[39m. References[0m                                                                       [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - This pattern matches common safe-extraction recommendations [0m[1;39m([0m[39me.g., OWASP[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m and [0m [2m                [0m
