[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m1[0m[39m. **Identify and correct off‐by‐one checks in literal parsing**  [0m                  [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   In `json_parse_ex` [0m[1;39m([0m[39msrc/json.c[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m, the branches handling the JSON literals “true”,[0m [2m                [0m
