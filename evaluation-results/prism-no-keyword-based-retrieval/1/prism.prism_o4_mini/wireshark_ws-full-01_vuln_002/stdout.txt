[2;36m                    [0m         [39m   - The global buffer pointer `hf_naws_data` is allocated in `dissect_naws_subopt`[0m [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39mthe pointer so that no subsequent code will use a stale pointer.[0m                    [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [39m     - In `telnet_sub_option` [0m[1;39m([0m[39maround line [0m[1;36m640[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m, change:[0m                            [2m                [0m

[2;36m                    [0m         [39m             [0m[1;35mwmem_free[0m[1;39m([0m[39mpinfo->pool, hf_naws_data[0m[1;39m)[0m[39m;[0m                                  [2m                [0m
