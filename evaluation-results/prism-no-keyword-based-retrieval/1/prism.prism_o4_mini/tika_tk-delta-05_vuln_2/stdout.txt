[2;36m                    [0m         [39mdependency.[0m                                                                         [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m                                                                                             [2m                [0m
[2;36m                    [0m         [1;36m6[0m[39m. Potential Side‚ÄêEffects & Mitigation[0m                                              [2m                [0m
